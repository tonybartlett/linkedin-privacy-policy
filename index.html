Privacy Policy — Tony LinkedIn Agent

Effective date: 2026-06-22

This Privacy Policy explains how Tony LinkedIn Agent (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and discloses information when you connect your LinkedIn account to the app and use related services.

Information we collect

  • LinkedIn profile information: profile ID, name, email address, and any other LinkedIn fields you authorize.
  • Authentication tokens: OAuth access tokens from LinkedIn needed to perform actions you explicitly approve (e.g., posting after approval).
  • Approval emails: email addresses used to route approval requests and responses for drafts and replies.

How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Draft LinkedIn posts and comment-reply suggestions using the permissions you grant.
  • Send approval emails and process Yes/No responses to publish approved content.
  • Troubleshoot and improve the service.

Data retention and deletion

We retain tokens and any stored drafts only as long as necessary to provide the approved service. You can revoke access by removing the app from your LinkedIn account (Account → Settings → Partners and Services) or by contacting us at the email below to request deletion of stored data.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your choices

You may revoke LinkedIn access at any time in your LinkedIn account settings. To request deletion of stored data or for other privacy concerns, contact: ynotbartlett@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or our practices, contact: ynotbartlett@gmail.com.
